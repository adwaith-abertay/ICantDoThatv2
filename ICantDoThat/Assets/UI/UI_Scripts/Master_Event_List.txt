--- MASTER EVENT CHEAT SHEET (v2.0) ---
HOW TO USE:

Copy and paste the line you need into your game logic.

"CharacterName" should be replaced with the string tag of the person involved (e.g., "Captain", "Scientist", "Soldier", "Engineer", "Robot", or "Injured").

Events listed as (GLOBAL) no longer require any parameters; the UIEventsListener will automatically pick a random survivor to react.  

1. GENERAL REACTIONS
A crew member gets frightened:
UIEventsListener.OnFrightened?.Invoke("CharacterName");

  

A crew member gets terrified (Greater Fear):
UIEventsListener.OnTerrified?.Invoke("CharacterName");

  

A door gets shut (GLOBAL):
UIEventsListener.OnDoorShut?.Invoke();

  

A fire starts (GLOBAL):
UIEventsListener.OnFireIgnited?.Invoke();

  

An airlock opens (GLOBAL):
UIEventsListener.OnAirlockOpened?.Invoke();

  

The robot gets hacked (GLOBAL):
UIEventsListener.OnRobotHacked?.Invoke();

  

The alien gets released (GLOBAL):
UIEventsListener.OnAlienReleased?.Invoke();

  

The oxygen gets deactivated (GLOBAL):
UIEventsListener.OnOxygenDeactivated?.Invoke();

  

2. CREW ACTIONS
(Pass the "CharacterName" of the person doing the action so they say their specific line)

A crew member starts disabling a C.A.P point:
UIEventsListener.OnCapDisabled?.Invoke("CharacterName");

  

A crew member picks up the axe:
UIEventsListener.OnAxePickedUp?.Invoke("CharacterName");

  

A crew member picks up the fire extinguisher:
UIEventsListener.OnExtinguisherPickedUp?.Invoke("CharacterName");

  

3. DEATH TRIGGERS (COMIC + TEXT)
When someone dies, this single line silences them permanently, plays their specific comic, and triggers a random survivor to react.

  

Killed by the Airlock:
UIEventsListener.OnCharacterDeath?.Invoke("CharacterName", "Airlock");

  

Killed by Fire:
UIEventsListener.OnCharacterDeath?.Invoke("CharacterName", "Fire");

  

Killed by the Alien:
UIEventsListener.OnCharacterDeath?.Invoke("CharacterName", "Alien");

  

Killed by the Hacked Robot:
UIEventsListener.OnCharacterDeath?.Invoke("CharacterName", "Hack");

  

4. AMBIENCE
When a crew member starts their turn:
UIEventsListener.OnTurnStarted?.Invoke("CharacterName");
