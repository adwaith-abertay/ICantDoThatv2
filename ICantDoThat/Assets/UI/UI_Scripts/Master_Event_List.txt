--- MASTER EVENT CHEAT SHEET ---

HOW TO USE:
Copy and paste the line you need into your game logic.
Replace "CharacterName" with the person the event is happening to 
e.g.

"Captain"

"Engineer"

"Scientist"

"Soldier"

"Injured" (Use this specifically for the injured version of the soldier)

"Robot"

"Alien"

The UI scripts system will handle the dice rolls and UI automatically.

--- 1. GENERAL REACTIONS ---
A crew member gets frightened:
GameEventManager.OnFrightened?.Invoke(GetRandomReactor());

A crew member gets terrified (Greater Fear):
GameEventManager.OnTerrified?.Invoke(GetRandomReactor());

A door gets shut:
GameEventManager.OnDoorShut?.Invoke(GetRandomReactor());

A fire starts:
GameEventManager.OnFireIgnited?.Invoke(GetRandomReactor());

An airlock opens:
GameEventManager.OnAirlockOpened?.Invoke(GetRandomReactor());

The robot gets hacked (Switches teams!):
GameEventManager.OnRobotHacked?.Invoke(GetRandomReactor());

The alien gets released:
GameEventManager.OnAlienReleased?.Invoke(GetRandomReactor());

The oxygen gets deactivated:
GameEventManager.OnOxygenDeactivated?.Invoke(GetRandomReactor());

--- 2. CREW ACTIONS ---
(Pass "CharacterName" here so the person doing the action says their specific Intention line!)

A crew member disables a C.A.P point:
GameEventManager.OnCapDisabled?.Invoke("CharacterName");

A crew member picks up the axe:
GameEventManager.OnAxePickedUp?.Invoke("CharacterName");

A crew member picks up the fire extinguisher:
GameEventManager.OnExtinguisherPickedUp?.Invoke("CharacterName");

--- 3. DEATH TRIGGERS (COMIC + TEXT) ---
When someone dies, this single line mutes them permanently, plays their comic, and makes a random survivor react.

Killed by the Airlock:
GameEventManager.OnCharacterDeath?.Invoke("CharacterName", "Airlock", GetRandomReactor());

Killed by Fire:
GameEventManager.OnCharacterDeath?.Invoke("CharacterName", "Fire", GetRandomReactor());

Killed by the Alien:
GameEventManager.OnCharacterDeath?.Invoke("CharacterName", "Alien", GetRandomReactor());

Killed by the Hacked Robot:
GameEventManager.OnCharacterDeath?.Invoke("CharacterName", "Hack", GetRandomReactor());

--- 4. AMBIENCE ---
When a crew member starts their turn:
GameEventManager.OnTurnStarted?.Invoke("CharacterName");
